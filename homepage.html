Malaysia

MALAYSIA is a Southeast Asian country occupying parts of the Malay Penisula and the island Borneo.

The federal constitutional monarchy consists of 13 states and 3 federal territories, separated by the South China Sea into two regions:
Peninsular Malaysia and Borneo's East Malaysia..

The capital, Kuala Lumpur, is home to colonial buildings, busy shopping districts such as Bukit Bintang and skyscrapers such as the iconic, 451m-tall Petronas Twin Towers.


Flag of Malaysia: Jalur Gemilang
  1. Colors:
    - Red: Courage
    - White: Purity and Integrity
    - Yellow: Royal
    - Blue: Unity, Peace and Harmony

  2. Design elements:
    - Crescent and Star: The crescent represents Islam, the official religion of Malaysia, while the star symbolizes the unity of the 13 states and the federal government.
    - Stripes: The 14 alternating red and white horizontal stripes represent the 13 states of Malaysia and the federal territories.
    - 14-Pointed Star: The 14 points on the star represent the unity between these entities.
